## About The Project

**Xenon** is designed for developers who love dark interfaces but still want a splash of vibrant, carefully-selected color. It provides a high-contrast, low-distraction environment with a focus on readability and modern aesthetics. The syntax highlighting is based on familiar color schemes, remapped to fit the unique Xenon accent palette.

## Included Themes

This extension packs five distinct theme variations to match your preference:

-   **Xenon**: The signature theme with a balanced and modern feel.
-   **OneXenon**: A remap of the classic One Dark palette.
-   **Xenokai**: A remap of the beloved Monokai color scheme.
-   **Xenongit**: A remap inspired by the GitHub Dark theme.
-   **Xenozed**: A remap based on the Solarized Dark philosophy.

## Installation

1.  Open the Zed editor.
2.  Navigate to `File` > `Settings` > `Extensions`.
3.  Search for **"Xenon"**.
4.  Click the `Install` button.
5.  Once installed, open the theme selector (`Cmd+K`, then `Cmd+T`) and choose your preferred Xenon variant.
